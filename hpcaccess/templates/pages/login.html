Welcome to HPC Access.

This website allows users of the BIH HPC cluster to

  • display information about their main work group,
  • list the projects they are a member of,
  • and see quota utilisation for all storage locations they have access to.

Additionally principal investigators and group delegates can

  • start a new group on the BIH HPC cluster,
  • add new group members,
  • create projects with dedicated and collaborative storage resources,
  • and make requests for additional group/project resources.
